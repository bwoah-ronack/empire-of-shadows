# empire-of-shadows
Empire of Shadows
Empire of Shadows is a dark, text-based 2D strategy simulation game where players rise from a small-time operator to the invisible ruler of a massive underground empire.

Set in a corrupt modern metropolis, players build power through businesses, influence, intelligence networks, media control, and strategic expansion while avoiding police crackdowns and rival organizations.

Every decision shapes the city.

Buy underground businesses, manipulate markets, bribe officials, spread propaganda, expand territories, and control the balance of power as the city reacts dynamically to your actions.

The game combines:

strategy
economic management
political influence
risk management
empire building
random world events

into a fast-paced simulation where survival depends on balancing profit, influence, and public attention.
